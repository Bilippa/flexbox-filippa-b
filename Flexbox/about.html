Why nendoroids?

We here at Nendoroid are especially fond of anime and games. We wanted to allow people to bring their favorite characters to life through the art of figurines. Nendoroids are unlike many figurines interactive in the sense that you as a Nendoroid owner can pose and change the Nendoroid to your liking, almost making it more like a childrens toy than a figurine. Nendoroids are also made of quite durable material. The material also makes the colours vibrant and true to that animated feeling.
